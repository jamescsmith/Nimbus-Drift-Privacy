Privacy Policy

Sky Shapes: Cloud Shape Escape — James C. Smith

Last Updated: April 5, 2026

Introduction

This privacy policy describes how James C. Smith ("we," "us," or "our"), handles information in connection with the mobile application Sky Shapes: Cloud Shape Escape ("the App"), available on the Google Play Store and Apple App Store.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

Information We Collect

Sky Shapes: Cloud Shape Escape is a puzzle game. We do not require account creation or login, and we do not directly collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number. We do not serve advertisements.

However, the App uses Google Firebase Analytics, which automatically collects certain usage and device data, including:

  • App usage data: Session duration, screens viewed, in-app events (such as level completions), app opens, and crashes.
  • Device information: Device model, operating system and version, screen size, language, and country/region (derived from IP address).
  • App instance identifiers: A randomly generated ID assigned to your app installation, used to count unique users. This is not tied to your personal identity.

This data is collected to help us understand how the App is used, identify technical issues, and improve the player experience. We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, precise location data, payment information, or any other personally identifiable information.

How We Use Collected Data

The analytics data collected through Firebase is used solely to:

  • Monitor app stability and fix crashes or bugs.
  • Understand which features and levels players engage with.
  • Improve overall app performance and user experience.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Locally Stored Data

The App stores your game progress (such as your current level and settings preferences) locally on your device using standard platform storage. This data is not transmitted to us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party service that may collect data:

  • Google Firebase Analytics: Provided by Google LLC. Firebase Analytics collects usage and device data as described above. Google's use of this data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy and the Firebase Data Privacy documentation.

The App is also distributed through the Google Play Store and Apple App Store, which may collect data independently in accordance with their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of Google and Apple.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of consent in your jurisdiction). The analytics data collected by Firebase does not identify individual users. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us at the email below, and we will take steps to delete such information.

Data Retention

Firebase Analytics data is retained for up to 14 months, after which it is automatically deleted. You can reset your app instance identifier at any time by uninstalling and reinstalling the App.

Data Security

We rely on Google Firebase's security infrastructure to protect analytics data. While no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, Firebase employs industry-standard security measures to safeguard the data it processes. Game progress stored locally on your device is protected by your device's own security measures.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data under laws such as the GDPR (EU/UK), CCPA/CPRA (California), or other applicable privacy regulations. These rights may include:

  • The right to access the data collected about you.
  • The right to request deletion of your data.
  • The right to opt out of data collection.

To exercise these rights or ask questions, please contact us at the email below. You can also reset your analytics identifier by uninstalling and reinstalling the App, or disable analytics data sharing through your device's privacy settings.
